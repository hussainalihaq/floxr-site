How Datfly fits into your week

From scattered spreadsheets to a single live workspace.

Start with the sheets you already have. Datfly ingests your existing business and HR spreadsheets and turns them into structured, connected tables you can actually trust.

1

Import your existing sheets

Upload Excel or Google Sheets for employees, leave, payroll inputs and more. We help you map columns and clean up duplicates in minutes.

2

Define the workflows once

Set rules for approvals, notifications and data ownership. For example, who approves leave, who gets onboarding tasks, and what must be completed before day one.

3

Let Datfly keep things in sync

As people join, move or leave, Datfly updates every linked table, dashboard and report — without you touching a single formula.

What you can automate first

Start with HR, expand to the rest of your ops.

Employee source of truth

One live employee record instead of five different versions. Roles, locations, contracts, equipment and documents in a single profile.

Onboarding & offboarding checklists

Auto‑generate checklists for every new hire. Notify IT, finance and managers, and track completion without chasing people in Slack.

Time off & approvals

Simple leave requests, manager approvals and automatic balance updates. Export clean data straight into payroll.

Policies & documents

Store signed contracts, policies and key HR docs next to the people they belong to, with the access controls you need.

Reporting without VLOOKUPs

Headcount, attrition, diversity and cost reports built from your live data – not static spreadsheet exports.

Export when you need to

Still need a spreadsheet? Export any view to CSV or Excel in a click, without breaking your underlying system.
