Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running within 1 hour. Connect your tools (Slack, Google Workspace), configure your onboarding template, and you're ready to go.
Do I need technical skills to use Floxr?
No coding required. Our interface is designed for HR teams. Just fill in employee details and click "Run Onboarding" - Floxr handles the rest.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption for all data. Your employee information is encrypted at rest and in transit, and we never share your data with third parties.
What tools does Floxr integrate with?
At launch, we integrate with Slack, Google Workspace, and email. More integrations (Microsoft 365, Notion, etc.) are coming based on customer feedback.
Can I cancel anytime?
